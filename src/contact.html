Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-6 months ahead for most destinations, and 6-12 months for peak-season trips to places like Japan during cherry blossom season or the Maldives during winter holidays.

What's included in your packages?

All Wanderly packages include round-trip flights, accommodation, daily breakfast, airport transfers, and guided experiences as described. Optional add-ons like spa treatments or upgrades are priced separately.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely. Every itinerary can be tailored to your dates, budget, interests, and pace. Simply share your vision using our contact form and we'll craft something bespoke.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 14 days before departure. Between 14-7 days, a 50% refund applies. Within 7 days, the package is non-refundable. We strongly recommend travel insurance for every booking.

Do you arrange group or corporate trips?

Yes — we handle groups from 6 to 60+ travelers, including family reunions, corporate retreats, and destination weddings. Contact us for a custom group quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and installment plans via Affirm or Klarna. A 20% deposit secures your booking, with the balance due 30 days before departure.
